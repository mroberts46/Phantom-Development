Welcome!

After being down for over a year now, I'm finally back up and running. As a birthday gift to myself, I've been determined to have this site up, responsive, and include an area to post some random creative writings. Well, here it is...FINALLY!

Unlike many other portfolio sites,this site is a constant work in progress and therefore may look different upon subsequent visits. Do not let this scare you or discourage you from making this a regular stopping place during surf through the web.

Made with Assemble, the static site generator for Node.js, Grunt.js and Yeoman.
