Welcome!

You've just stumbled upon the NEW virtual playground for aspiring front-end developer, Marc Roberts!

As a deeveloper in these modern, technological times, it's important that to stay abreasted of the changes in the industry. Equally important is having a place to practice applying new industry standards in a real-world environment. What good is knowing that there's an easier way of doing something if you don't know how to apply the shortcut effectively.

That's why I created Phantom Development. This site serves two very important purposes. First, and most importantly, this site is for marketing my unique skill-set to potential employers. Second, this site is my workshop. As freelance developer, I can't always use a client's project as a practice piece. Time restraints and other restrictions often mean that I have to get a site up and running quickly. That doesn't leave much room for trial and error testing. Therefore, I practice with and on my own site to continue to grow my skill-set without the hassle of restrictions.

So if you find something that is broken or just doesn't work quite right, contact me and let me know. Usually, it's simply because I'm working on it at that particular time but there maay also be a chance that I'm unaware that it's not working. With that said, WELCOME TO MY PLAYGROUND!
